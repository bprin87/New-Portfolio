About Me

My journey into web development began eight years ago when I started teaching myself how to build static web pages using HTML and CSS. Fast forward a few years and I returned to university to study Web Design & Development, where I gained a deeper understanding of the technologies and principles behind building engaging, user-friendly websites and applications.

My main focus is front-end development—creating clean, responsive, and user-friendly interfaces—but I also have some experience on the back end, mainly with SQL. I enjoy exploring new frameworks and libraries to enhance both the creativity and responsiveness of the websites I build, and I am always excited to keep learning and pushing my skills further.
